How to Apply

01

Request Application Form

Request application form, stating your program, age, date of birth and other personal details.

02

Send Documents

Submit your filled application form to the school's admission office either physically or through mail for processing after which aspirants are to pay a non-refundable fee for entrance examination.

03

Examination Process

Aspirants with the proper documents and payment are invited to partake in an entrance examination to test their knowledge.

04

Decision Letter

Choosen students are sent letter of admission stating all important details. After this stage, the enrollment process is all over.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed Application Form
  • Official High School Transcripts
  • Junior WAEC Result (Optional for Secondary Aspirants Only)
  • Two Letters of Recommendation
  • Personal Essay (500-650 words)
  • Examination Fee (₦2,499 non-refundable)
  • Examination

Promotional class students require additional documentation including Common Entrance/Junior WAEC results.
