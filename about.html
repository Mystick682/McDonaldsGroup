Meet Us

Educating Minds, Inspiring Hearts

Our school motto, 'Respect' includes qualities such as kindness, compassion and consideration; while 'Responsibility'; is based upon honesty excellence, obedience, duty and sustainability. Several symbols are enclosed in the four quadrants; each with a symbol:

The Feather Quill

Quills are traditional writing instruments for scholars, philosophers, and writers, embodying wisdom and the acquisition of knowledge.

The Castle

Castles represent power, control and wealth; they also represent ambition and aspiration.

The Key

A key is the means to open locked doors, symbolizing new chances, a bright future, and access to new experiences or careers.

The River

Just as a river is always flowing and changing, it represents the continuous ad ever changing nature of life itself, the constant passage of experiences, and the broader sweep of history.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support the development of the whole child, intellectually, emotionally, socially, and physically; through dynamic partnership among staffs, families and the greater community.

Our Vision

1. Provide high quality care and education for all of our children.

2. Coordinate children's learning, using a broad-based, balanced and qualitative foundation stage and national curriculums.
